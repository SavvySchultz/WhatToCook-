  • Cheese
  • Tuna

Carbs

Give you energy for the day.

  • Bread
  • Rice
  • Pasta
  • Oats
  • Potatoes

Healthy Fats

Support the brain and help meals feel satisfying.

  • Avocado
  • Olive oil
  • Nuts
  • Peanut butter
  • Seeds

Fruits & Veggies

Add fiber, color, vitamins, and crunch.

  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
